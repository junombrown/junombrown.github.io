i am a dutch / english designer / artist currently based in eindhoven, netherlands.

right at this moment I am graduating from design academy eindhoven in the department leisure.

my work stems from an interest in material and spatial details, with a particular love for the unintentional scenography created in our daily surroundings. the subconscious actions and routines people undertake that make a space theirs, that shapes their room, house, city. if you look closely, there’s wisps of surrealism tucked away in every corner!

i am also keen to be able to do everything. i want to be a carpenter and a train driver and a chef and a scenographer and more. i am immersed currently and futurely in a lifelong process of learning it all.

feel free to get in contact for any reason at all.

transitioning through states - liquid to solid - plaster takes on and preserves the form of its surroundings

with this self developed technique, i explored plaster’s transient qualities; creating objects by casting in sewn canvas moulds; freely arranging whilst still wet by making use of the liquid state of the plaster and the flexibility of the textile
